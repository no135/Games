The Sovereign Legacy

Rule with honor, maintain your public image, and secure the crown.

👑 The Royal Mandate

1. Public Service

Perform at least 3 royal duties per year. Your approval rating must never drop below 80%.

2. Noble Lineage

You must produce at least one heir to continue the dynasty. Adoption does not count for this specific challenge route.

3. International Relations

Travel to a different country every 5 years to maintain global influence. No "shady" vacations allowed.

⚠️ The Exile Clause

You will be disqualified and "Exiled" if:

  • ❌ Execution: You execute a citizen for no reason.
  • ❌ Scandal: You are caught in a public affair or crime.
  • ❌ Low Favor: Public respect hits 0%.

🏆 Victory: Rule for 50 consecutive years without being abdicated.
